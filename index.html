Profile

About Dr. Dalvi

Dr. Madhukar H. Dalvi is a highly accomplished academician and research statistician with over 30 years of teaching and mentoring excellence in Mathematics, Statistics, and Operations Research at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels.

He has established a distinguished reputation as a workshop resource person and research consultant, having guided 83 researchers across institutions in the design, execution, and analysis of research data using contemporary statistical software including SPSS and jamovi.

His research spans transportation problems, Markov chain models, Data Envelopment Analysis, regression, factor analysis, and applied statistics, with publications in Scopus-indexed, UGC CARE Listed, and peer-reviewed journals.

Dr. Dalvi served as Resource Person at 16 ICSSR-sponsored national workshops on research methodology, and has chaired sessions at 2 International and 2 National Level Conferences. He completed a UGC-Funded Minor Research Project and contributes actively to faculty development through UGC Refresher and Short-Term Courses.
